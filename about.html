What is Crocheting?

Crocheting is a type of needlework craft that uses a hook to create fabric from loops of yarn. You can make so many different things with it such as blankets, clothes, decorations, and more!

What You Will Need!

  • Yarn
  • Crochet Hooks
  • Stitch Markers
  • Scissors
  • Needles

How Do You Crochet?

  1. Choose your yarn and your hook size
  2. Watch a video or read a book about the basic stitches
  3. Choose a small project to start on
